Schedule

This website, content, and videos are created as part of Lily's research project on the effectiveness of a girl-centric, project-based Computer Science curriculum using Scratch on encouraging 3rd - 5th grade girls' participation in computing.

Below is the list of important dates, if you would like to participate in Lily's Masters in Computer Science research project: Coding With Lily!

Note that participation is voluntary as are all meetings on Zoom are optional.

Remote Meetings

All meetings will be on Zoom. Links to the Zoom meetings will be made available two days prior to the meeting date and sent to the parent's email address.
